Irina Spicaka

Hello, my name is Irina Spicaka. I'm Latvian graphic designer, media artist, curator currently based in Berlin and always open to relocate due to work related issues. Co-founder of the CC4AV platform and small media production studio CodeBark. Besides that, I write about art, business and technology and hold projection mapping workshops.
